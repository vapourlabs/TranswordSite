What is Transword?

The future of all translation bots on Discord. Of the many first for its own kind, Transword's purpose is to be that bridge that connects the gaps together between language barriers with users. Using the top-of-the-line services provided by DeepL, you will be able to know for certain that Transword's translating skills will be kept authentic and as close to being correct as humanly possible. While Transword will not be perfect, nor' will it ever be, it will always remain to be far better than other translation services currently offered out there.



How does it work?

Transword works off of Discord's Slash Command UI design, making this the biggest part of the flagship for the bot. Likewise, Transword utilizes many new features of the Slash Command design to its advantage, such as the options formatting, the choices dropdown list, ease of access with the forward-slash / character, and even more!
